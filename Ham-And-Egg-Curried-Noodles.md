---
title: Ham & Egg Curried Noodles
source: 5 Ingredients by Jamie Oliver
serves: 2
time: 10 Minutes
---
## Ingredients
- 5 oz egg noodles
- 4 scallions
- 3½ oz roast ham
- 2 teaspoons curry powder
- 2 large eggs

## Steps

Cook the noodles in a pan of boiling salted water according to the package
instructions, then drain, reserving a cupful of cooking water. Meanwhile, trim
and finely slice the scallions and finely slice the ham.

Place the ham in a non-stick frying pan on a medium-high heat with 1 tablespoon
of olive oil and the curry powder. While it gets nicely golden, beat the eggs
and pour them into the pan, moving them around with a rubber spatula until they
start to cook, then stir in the noodles and a good splash of the scallions. Toss
over the heat for 2 minutes, then taste and season to perfection with sea salt
and black pepper, loosening with a splash of reserved noodle water, if needed.
Dish up the noodles, scatter over the remaining scallions, and finish with 1
teaspoon of extra virgin olive oil.
