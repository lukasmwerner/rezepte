---
title: Sesame Shiitake Egg Noodles
source: 5 Ingredients by Jamie Oliver
serves: 2
time: 10 Minutes
---
## Ingredients
- 4 oz shiitake mushrooms
- 5 oz egg noodles
- 3 cloves of garlic
- 3 tablespoons raw sesame seeds
- 3 tablespoons kecap manis

## Steps

Halve the mushrooms, then dry toast in a large non-stick frying pan on a
medium-high heat for 8 minutes, or until golden and nutty, tossing occasionally.
Meanwhile, cook the noodles in a pan of boiling salted water according to the
package instructions, then drain, reserving a cupful of cooking water. Peel and
finely slice the garlic, add to the mushroom pan with 1 tablespoon of olive oil
and cook for a further 2 minutes, tossing regularly. 

Pound the sesame seeds until fine in a pestle and mortar, then toss into the pan
with the noodles, kecap manis, and a good splash of reserved noodle water until
everything is well coated. Taste, season to perfection with sea salt and black
pepper, if needed, and dish right up.
