---
title: Italian Baked Rice
source: 5 Ingredients by Jamie Oliver
serves: 4
time: Fast Prep 10 Minutes, Cook 40 Minutes
---
## Ingredients
- 2 onions
- 2 oz fennel salami
- 1½ cups Arborio risotto rice
- 1 heaping tablespoon mascarpone cheese
- 1½ oz Parmesan cheese

## Steps

Preheat the oven to 350°F. Place a large shallow casserole pan on a high heat.
Peel and quarter the onions and quickly break apart into petals straight into
the pan. Chop for 4 minutes, tossing regularly. Reduce to a medium heat and stir
in 1 tablespoon of olive oil and the salami, then the rice, followed 1 minute
later by 5 cups of boiling kettle water and the mascarpone, finely grate and
stir in the Parmesan with a pinch of sea salt and black pepper. 

Transfer the pan to the oven for 40 minutes, or until the rice has absorbed the
liquid and is just cooked through. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of extra virgin
olive oil and dish up, seasoning to perfection at the table.
