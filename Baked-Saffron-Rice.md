---
title: Baked Saffron Rice
source: 5 Ingredients by Jamie Oliver
serves: 4
time: 26 Minutes
---
## Ingredients
- 2 red onions
- 2 small pinches of saffron
- 4 heaping tablespoons plain yogurt
- 4 tablespoons sun-dried tomato paste
- 1 1/3 cups white basmati rice

## Steps

Preheat the oven to 400°F. Peel and finely chop the red onions. Place a 12-x 10-inch
roasting pan on a high heat on the stove, pour in I tablespoon of olive oil,
add the onions, and fry for 4 minutes, or until soft and sweet, stirring regularly.
Meanwhile, place half the saffron in 2½ cups of boiling kettle water. In a bowl,
cover the remaining saffron with I tablespoon of boiling water, steep for
10 seconds, then mix with the yogurt and put aside.

Stir the tomato paste, rice, and a pinch of sea salt and black pepper into the
onion pan, then pour in the saffron water and bring to a boil. Once boiling,
carefully transfer to the oven for 15 minutes, or until the rice has absorbed all
the liquid, fluffed up beautifully, and is golden and crisp on top.

Spoon the saffron yogurt over the rice, drizzle it all with I tablespoon of extra
virgin olive oil, fork and mix it all together, and dish up.
