---
title: Hoisin Bok Choy Rice
source: 5 Ingredients by Jamie Oliver
serves: 2
time: 15 Minutes
---
## Ingredients
- ¾ cup white basmati rice
- 4 scallions
- 2 fresh medium-color chilies
- 2 bok choy (8 oz)
- 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce

## Steps

Cook the rice in a pan of boiling salted water according to the package
instructions, then drain. Meanwhile, trim and finely slice the scallions,
pricking the white slices into - bowl. Seed and finely chop the chilies, add to
the bowl with 1 tablespoon of red-wine vinegar and a little sea salt and black
pepper, and mix well to make a dressing. 

Halve the bok choy lengthways and place in a non-stick frying pan on a
medium-high heat with 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Once charred, toss in the green
scallion slices, then stir in the hoisin. Let it bubble for 1 minute, then in
the rice for 1 final minute. Spoon over the chili dressing and serve up.
