## Ingredients

- Bread
- Cheese

## Steps

Slice two thin slices of bread. Coat both sides of each slice with butter. Cut cheese, and put on pan. It is a grilled cheese sandwich, come on.

## Variations

Replace butter with mayo, can be cheaper.
