---
title: Black Tahini Noodles
source: 5 Ingredients by Jamie Oliver
serves: 2
time: 15 Minutes
---
## Ingredients
- 5 oz fine rice noodles
- 2 limes
- 1 cup sprouting cress
- 1¾ oz raw black sesame seeds
- 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce

## Steps

Cook the noodles in boiling salted water according to the package instructions,
then drain, reserving a cupful of cooking water. Meanwhile, finely grate the
zest of 1 lime, snip the cress, and put both aside. Toast the sesame seeds in a
dry non-stick frying pan on a high heat for 1 minute, tossing regularly.
Reserving one-quarter of the seeds, pound the rest in a pestle and mortar until
fairly fine, then muddle in the teriyaki and the juice of 1 lime. Taste, season
to perfection with sea salt and black pepper, and you've got a black tahini!

Toss the noodles and black tahini together, loosening with a splash of reserved
noodle water. Serve sprinkled with the lime zest, cress, and reserved seeds,
with lime wedges on the side, for squeezing over.
